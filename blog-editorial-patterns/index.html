Recommended scaffold

Treat the blog as an editorial column. Three intents:

  1. Clean, light, sans-only. Inter or aeonik headings, IBM Plex Sans body, neutral grays. No serifs, no brand-yellow chrome, no dark cards.
  2. Authoring stays dead-simple. A new post is: drop an .mdx into src/content/blog/, fill frontmatter, done. The MDX components map handles styling.
  3. Plan for growth without over-engineering today. Featured 2-up grid + all-posts grid below is enough until ~8 posts. Then add Cursor's tabular fallback.
